    Our steps to make you a great dancer

    We make a base line about your physical condition to schedule a plan suited for your needs and for the dance you want to learn.

    We develop specifically the exercises for you to advance in progressively in dancing and avoid lessons.

    It's important to practice in your home to become a great dancer, for that reason we design a plan suited for you based on the condition analysis.

    We make group sessions for you to know people and apply your skills earned at home.
